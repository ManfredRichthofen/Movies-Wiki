# YouTube Music for Android

Ad-free music streaming with background playback — installed as an APK on your Android device.

**New here?** Follow the [installation guide](/docs/installation). If you already installed **MicroG** for YouTube, skip straight to downloading **YouTube Music** from the [Apps page](/Apps).

## What you get

- No ads between songs
- Background playback
- Picture-in-picture
- Offline-friendly listening (where supported)

## Extra troubleshooting

**Can’t sign in**

- Confirm **MicroG** is installed and you’re signed in there first.

**App won’t update**

- Download the latest **YouTube Music** APK from the [Apps page](/Apps) and install it over the old version.

## Updates

Check the [Apps page](/Apps) for new builds when playback or sign-in stops working.
