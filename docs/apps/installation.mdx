# Installing YouTube on Android

This guide walks you through installing **ad-free YouTube** and **YouTube Music** on an Android phone or tablet. You will download three files and install them in a specific order.

:::info What you need
- An **Android phone or tablet** (Android 8.0 or newer)
- About **200 MB** of free storage
- A few minutes — no computer required
:::

## Step 1 — Download the apps

Open the [Apps page](/Apps) on your phone and download these files:

1. **MicroG** — required helper app (install this **first**)
2. **YouTube** and/or **YouTube Music** — pick one or both

Your browser will save `.apk` files. You can find them in your **Downloads** folder or by opening your browser’s download list.

## Step 2 — Allow installs from your browser

Android blocks apps from outside the Play Store until you allow it once.

1. Open **Settings**
2. Search for **Install unknown apps** (wording varies by phone)
3. Tap your **browser** (Chrome, Samsung Internet, etc.)
4. Turn on **Allow from this source**

You only need to do this once for the browser you used to download the APKs.

## Step 3 — Install MicroG

1. Open your **Downloads** folder (or tap the finished download in your browser)
2. Tap the **MicroG** file
3. Tap **Install** — allow unknown apps if asked
4. When it finishes, tap **Open**
5. Grant any permissions it asks for and allow it to **run in the background**

:::warning Keep MicroG installed
Do not uninstall MicroG later. YouTube and YouTube Music need it to sign in and play in the background.
:::

## Step 4 — Install YouTube or YouTube Music

1. Go back to **Downloads**
2. Tap the **YouTube** or **YouTube Music** file
3. Tap **Install**

:::tip Already have YouTube installed?
Uninstall the regular YouTube app from the Play Store first, or the new app may fail to install.
:::

## Step 5 — Sign in and test

1. Open **YouTube** (or **YouTube Music**)
2. Sign in with your **Google account**
3. Play a video or song — there should be **no ads**
4. Lock your phone — audio should **keep playing**

If sign-in fails, open **MicroG**, make sure you’re signed in there, restart your phone, and try again.

## Something went wrong?

| What you see | What to try |
| --- | --- |
| Install blocked | Turn on “Install unknown apps” for your browser (Step 2) |
| “App not installed” | Uninstall stock YouTube/YouTube Music, free up storage, download fresh APKs from [/Apps](/Apps) |
| Sign-in loop | Reinstall MicroG, sign in inside MicroG first, then open YouTube |
| No background audio | Settings → Battery → set **MicroG** and **YouTube** to **Unrestricted** |
| App crashes | Download the latest APKs from [/Apps](/Apps) — old builds stop working when YouTube updates |

Need more help? See the [YouTube](/docs/apps/youtube) and [YouTube Music](/docs/apps/youtube-music) pages for extra tips.
